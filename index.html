The Four Seasons 四季色彩

Which season are you?

Personal colour analysis identifies the palette of hues that naturally complement your skin, hair, and eyes. Every person falls into one of four seasonal colour types.

Spring

春 ・ スプリング

Warm, bright, and fresh. Spring types glow in coral, peach, warm gold, and clear greens.

Summer

夏 ・ サマー

Cool, soft, and elegant. Summer types shine in lavender, dusty rose, powder blue, and mauve.

Autumn

秋 ・ オータム

Warm, deep, and rich. Autumn types flourish in terracotta, olive, mustard, and chocolate.

Winter

冬 ・ ウィンター

Cool, vivid, and striking. Winter types command attention in black, white, jewel tones, and icy hues.
